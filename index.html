Rental Logistics:

  • Pick-up Time: After 2PM on the day before your first shoot day.
  • Drop-off Time: Before NOON on the day after your last shoot day.
  • Billable Days: We bill for number of SHOOT days, maxing out at three in a week.
  • Weekends: Saturdays and Sundays are counted as one shoot day. Therefore, pickup on a Friday and dropoff on a Monday will be charged as one shoot day.
